About Seriti Insights


A 100 per cent Motswana owned boutique Data Analytics and Inteligence firm, making us disruptive in this respect. Seriti Insights is an Industry Agnostic, B2B with a goal to connect enterprises in Botswana to the possibilities of Data. Seriti Insights is focused on providing tailored data analytics & data centred solutions and generating intelligence which address diverse problems facing leaders in their organisations.

We Operate in context where Big Data is changing our world rapidly and its impacting how we conduct business in today's world. Every Business today needs to find a way to deal with Big Data -- how to process it, how to use it and how to monetize it. In this ever-changing environment, it is crucial for businesses to adapt quickly and intelligently ahead of competitors to ensure their sustainability in the medium to long term.


Leadership


  • Kagiso Earl Mpa
               CTO
  • Nomsa Makgabenyana
               Founder

Before stepping into Entrepreneurship, and Seriti Insights in late 2017, Nomsa spent some time at the Botswana Trade and Investment Centre performing a Business Diagnostics role. Her greatest motivation when starting up Seriti Insights, was vision to do meaningful work with African companies using their own data potential to achieve their bottom lines. In doing so creating better ecosystems. Nomsa is passionate about Business Intelligence, it is a means to the more valuable end: people. Her greatest passion is leaders of the organisations helping them to successfully shape their organisations by tapping into the possibilities of Data. Ms Makgabenyana is responsible for building the start-up into an asset of value, Lead to Deal conversion of the firm, Engagement management and strengthening Seriti Insights brand and reach across various industries. Nomsa Makgabenyana is a two time University of Cape Town Graduate, and has a certification in Data Analytics, also from UCT. She also serves a non-executive Board member for a financial advisory company.

Kagiso Mpa is a Data Scientist with hands on experience in handling and analyzing enterprise big data; data management, data mining, data visualization, customer experience (market research) and business intelligence. Before joining Seriti Insights, Kagiso had worked for Botswana Life Insurance as an Actuarial Assistant, and later joined Alpha Direct Insurance as a Data Analyst. To pursue his analytics passion, Kagiso went on to further his studies in Data Analytics and Business Intelligence, while also working as a Business Intelligence Analyst at University of the Western Cape.
